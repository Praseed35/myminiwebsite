Welcome to Our Website

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company that designs, manufactures, and sells consumer electronics, software, and services. The company was founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, with the headquarters located in Cupertino, California, USA.

Products and Services: Apple is renowned for its wide range of consumer electronics, software, and services. Some of its flagship products include the iPhone (smartphone), iPad (tablet), Mac (personal computer), Apple Watch (smartwatch), and Apple TV (media streaming device). It also offers a variety of software and services like iOS and macOS operating systems, the App Store, iCloud, iTunes, Apple Music, and Apple Pay.
